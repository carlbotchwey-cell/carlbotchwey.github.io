Welcome!

My name is Carl Botchwey. I am a PhD Candidate in Accounting at Université Clermont Auvergne.

My research interests broadly encompass the reporting and dissemination of corporate information in capital markets, with a particular focus on earnings management, analyst forecast behavior, institutional environments, and sustainability reporting.

Before beginning my PhD, I earned a Master of Commerce (Accounting) and a Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting) — both from the University of Cape Coast, Ghana — graduating with distinction.

I am also an Associate Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants, Ghana.
